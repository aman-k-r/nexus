NEXUS'24

About Us


Welcome to NEXUS'24, the tech extravaganza of Government Engineering College, Samastipur! Organized by ASIMO: The Technical Club of GEC Samastipur, NEXUS'24 is set to electrify your senses with two unforgettable days of innovation and excitement on August 15 and 16, 2024.

NEXUS'24 is not just an event; it's a celebration of technological brilliance and creative genius. This fully self-financed and sponsored techfest highlights the passion and ingenuity of our students, fueled by the generous support of our sponsors. Get ready to dive into 14 thrilling events, from adrenaline-pumping coding competitions and hackathons to mind-bending robotics challenges and enlightening tech talks, all designed to ignite your curiosity and challenge your intellect.

What makes NEXUS'24 truly special is its vibrant community spirit. It's a melting pot where ideas collide, creativity flourishes, and innovation knows no bounds. Students from all disciplines come together to share knowledge, foster creativity, and build lasting friendships. At NEXUS'24, we believe that when bright minds unite, extraordinary things happen.

Join us at NEXUS'24 and immerse yourself in a whirlwind of excitement and discovery. Whether you're a tech wizard, an aspiring engineer, or simply eager to explore the latest technological marvels, NEXUS'24 promises an experience like no other. Mark your calendars for August 15-16, 2024, and be part of an extraordinary journey where technology and inspiration converge. Welcome to NEXUS'24 - where the future unfolds!

Frequently Asked Questions

FAQs

+ How do I register for NEXUS'24 ?
To register for NEXUS'24, visit our official website and click on the "Register Now" button. Follow the prompts to fill in your details and complete the registration process.
+ Is there a registration fee for participating in NEXUS'24?
Yes, there is a nominal registration fee to participate in NEXUS'24. The fee covers access to all events and activities over the two days. Detailed fee information is available on the registration page.
+ Can I participate in multiple events with a single registration?
Absolutely! With a single registration, you can participate in multiple events at NEXUS'24. However, please ensure that the events do not overlap in timing.
+ What is the deadline for registration ?
The deadline for registration is August 13, 2024. We encourage early registration to secure your spot and avoid any last-minute hassles.
+ Will I receive a confirmation after registering ?
Yes, once you complete the registration process, you will receive a confirmation email with your registration details and further instructions.
+ Are there any additional charges for specific events ?
While the general registration fee covers most events, some specialized workshops or competitions may have additional charges. Details of any additional fees will be provided on the event page
+ What do I need to bring on the day of the techfest ?
Please bring a copy of your registration confirmation email, a valid student ID, and any materials required for your specific events. Additional details will be provided in your confirmation email.
